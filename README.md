# Uniting Users (UU)

**UU** is the creator and steward of the **UU Protocol**, introducing a new primitive for the internet: **Proof of Human Origin**.

The protocol enables digital systems to recognize human participation without identity databases, personal data collection, or storage.  
All mechanisms—including **Human-Origin Proofs (HOPs)**, **Proof-of-Humanity (POH)**, and **Proof-of-Identity (POI)**—are designed to be **immutable, privacy-preserving, and neutral**.

## Mission

UU’s mission is to ensure the internet can distinguish human participation from automated or synthetic activity while preserving:

- Privacy-first design  
- Permissionless access  
- Neutral, immutable infrastructure  

The Human-Origin Layer prevents misuse for data harvesting, tracking, or behavioral exploitation.

## Research & Origins

UU builds upon extensive research conducted by **LinksUU Technology Solutions LTD (UK, 2018–2025)**, which served as the foundational R&D environment for the development of a storage-free trust architecture.  
UU Protocol formally launched as a protocol in **2025**.

### Key Articles

1. **HOPs – Internet Primitive Thesis**: Foundational theory establishing the Human-Origin Layer as a new internet primitive.  
2. **Human-Origin Network**: Architectural design for privacy-preserving, permissionless HOP signal delivery and ecosystem integration.  
3. **The $1T Problem**: Analysis of the economic and behavioral risks posed by AI impersonation, synthetic identity proliferation, and large-scale tracking, highlighting the systemic need for UU.
