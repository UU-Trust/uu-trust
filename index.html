UU

UU is building a new trust primitive for the post-AI world.

At its core lies Self-Certifying Cryptographic Trust — an architecture that enables Proof-of-Humanity (PoH) and Proof-of-Identity (PoI) without storing identity, personal data, biometrics, or long-term records.

Trust is established cryptographically, momentarily, and without surveillance.

What UU Enables

  • Verification of real human presence without identity exposure
  • Authentication without accounts, databases, or long-term storage
  • Resistance to AI impersonation, bots, and synthetic identities
  • Trust-minimized verification for platforms, protocols, and institutions
