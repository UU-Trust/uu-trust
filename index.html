Uniting Users (UU)

Steward of the UU Protocol
Privacy-first, Neutral, Permissionless Internet Infrastructure

UU is building a new trust primitive for the post-AI world. At its core lies the Human-Origin Layer—an architecture enabling Proof-of-Humanity (POH) and Proof-of-Identity (POI) without storing identity, personal data, biometrics, or long-term records. Trust is established momentarily, cryptographically, and without surveillance.

All protocol mechanisms—including Human-Origin Proofs (HOPs), POH, and POI—are immutable, privacy-preserving, and neutral. The UU Protocol was developed from extensive research conducted between 2018–2025 under LinksUU Technology Solutions LTD (UK), which provided the foundational experimentation and architectural explorations that led to the current storage-free, permissionless trust architecture. UU itself formally launched as a protocol in 2025.

What UU Enables

  • Recognition of real human participation without identity exposure
  • Authentication without accounts, databases, or long-term storage
  • Resistance to AI impersonation, bots, and synthetic identities
  • Trust-minimized interactions for platforms, protocols, and institutions
  • Immutable Human-Origin Layer that prevents misuse for data harvesting or behavioral tracking

Research & Technical Writing

UU’s conceptual foundations and technical design are documented in three major research outputs:

  • HOPs – Internet Primitive Thesis: Foundational theory establishing the Human-Origin Layer as a new internet primitive.
  • Human-Origin Network: Architectural design for privacy-preserving, permissionless HOP signal delivery and ecosystem integration.
  • The $1T Problem: Analysis of the economic and behavioral risks posed by AI impersonation, synthetic identity proliferation, and large-scale tracking, highlighting the systemic need for UU.

These outputs build directly on the extensive deep-tech research conducted by LinksUU Technology Solutions LTD (UK, 2018–2025), which served as the long-term R&D environment from which UU emerged.
