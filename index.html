Steward of the UU Protocol

Privacy-first, Neutral, Permissionless Internet Infrastructure

UU is building a new trust primitive for the post-AI world. At its core lies the Human-Origin Layer—an architecture enabling Proof-of-Humanity (POH) and Proof-of-Identity (POI) without storing identity, personal data, biometrics, or long-term records. Trust is established momentarily, cryptographically, and without surveillance.

All protocol mechanisms—including Human-Origin Proofs (HOPs), POH, and POI—are immutable, privacy-preserving, and neutral. The UU Protocol was developed from extensive research conducted between 2018–2025 under LinksUU Technology Solutions LTD (UK), which provided the foundational experimentation and architectural explorations that led to the current storage-free, permissionless trust architecture. UU Protocol itself formally launched as a protocol in 2026.

Learn more about the protocol: https://uu-protocol.org

What UU Enables

Recognition of real human participation without identity exposure
Authentication without accounts, databases, or long-term storage
Resistance to AI impersonation, bots, and synthetic identities
Trust-minimized interactions for platforms, protocols, and institutions
Immutable Human-Origin Layer that prevents misuse for data harvesting or behavioral tracking
